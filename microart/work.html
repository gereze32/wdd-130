About

Work

Part of his secret is to get into a lot of concentration, lower his heart rate and breathing to the minimum possible level to the point of practically not moving, and finally, work between heartbeats since it is the only vibration impossible to avoid in the pulse of the hand. The technique that he uses is unique since it is simply a talent developed with a lot of practice and a lot of patience. In fact, tools such as cutters, paints, sculpting materials or tweezers are all created by the artist and in many cases for each new idea, the artist must investigate, try different materials, create new tools, and discover new things in each new project.

Concentration

The small works often made on pinheads and even written tabs are so small that any sudden movement or strong breath can ruin the work, break it or disappear, so the artist's concentration is one of the most necessary tools to work.

Polo Jerez has come to understand that in such a tiny world, materials behave differently and every little detail is a very small detail. a little sigh and the work can disappear in from the artist's eyes! Among the limited exhibitions that have been made of the collections, the impact generated in the visitors is always amazement and the repeated phrase among them: "I have seen something unique, and incredible that I will never forget"

Average size of works
One square millimeter

Polo Jerez has challenged itself to maintain an average size per work or parts of them of one square millimeter or less.

Experience has shown us that it is very difficult for people to understand what the works are about, since they have generally never seen anything similar.
